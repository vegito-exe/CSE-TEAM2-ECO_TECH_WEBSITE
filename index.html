ABOUT

Eco Tech is a two-day event organized by the CSE club, taking place at ESI Algiers from Friday, November 26 to Saturday, November 27. It features an AI-centered hackathon focused on solving environmental challenges. The experience begins with advanced online workshops and mini-projects in AI, Development, and UI/UX, preparing participants for an exciting, collaborative, and creativity-driven competition.

The CSE Club of ESI Algiers is one of Algeria's leading university clubs. Founded in 2008 by students of the National Higher School of Computer Science (ESI) in Algiers, its mission is to energize student life and encourage the sharing and exchange of knowledge in the technological field. Through its wide range of activities, the club aims to offer students a space where learning and enjoyment come together seamlessly.

FAQs

What is Eco Tech?
Eco Tech Week is a dynamic two-day event organized by CSE, centered around a hackathon where participants use Artificial Intelligence (AI) to develop solutions for pressing ecological challenges. The hackathon is preceded by a series of five advanced workshops and three mini-projects to equip participants with the necessary skills.
When and where will it take place?
The event takes place from Friday, November 28 to Saturday, November 29, 2025, at ESI Alger, in Oued Smar, Algeria.
Who can participate?
The event is open to anyone who meets the prerequisites! You can register as a pre-formed team or as an individual. Individual participants will be grouped into balanced teams to ensure a mix of essential skills: AI, Development, and UI/UX.
What is the main goal of the event?
The main goal of Eco Tech Week is to solve ecological problems and help improve the environment by providing a platform for participants to enhance their technical skills, network with like-minded individuals, and strengthen their problem-solving, creativity, and teamwork abilities, all while showcasing emerging talent.
How can I register or get involved?
You can register online through our website or contact us for sponsorship and volunteer opportunities.
