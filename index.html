ABOUT

Eco Tech is a two-day event organized by the CSE club, taking place at ESI Algiers from Friday, November 26 to Saturday, November 27. It features an AI-centered hackathon focused on solving environmental challenges. The experience begins with advanced online workshops and mini-projects in AI, Development, and UI/UX, preparing participants for an exciting, collaborative, and creativity-driven competition.

At the CSE Club in ESI Algiers, we’re all about tech, challenges, and learning. We’re a community of passionate tech enthusiasts who love to dive into new technologies and tackle problems together, growing and sharing knowledge along the way.
